RETAIL + ENTERTAINMENT

t’s about the experience. Creating unique destinations where consumers are engaged and have choice. Today’s retail environments are rapidly evolving, taking cues from the hospitality, entertainment and corporate worlds to provide variety, create connections and ultimately entice consumers to shop offline. The blurred lines aren’t new to us. Retail experts for more than 40 years, RSP has continually evolved our planning and design approach in lock-step with our clients. Developers, national retailers or single-store owners. No matter the client, we make the effort to understand the brand, the business and desired experience. Only then can we offer a team of experts capable of delivering the project’s goals. The diversity of our practice includes expertise in the entire supply chain, plus hospitality, housing, healthcare and corporate – a growing need when planning dynamic entertainment-driven communities. Yet we never lose sight of ultimate goal. At the end of the day, it’s still about making the cash register ring.
